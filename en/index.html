Unleash Courses

Empowering Future Professionals Through Skill-Based Learning

Center for Professional Development (CPDSkill) is a next-generation training institution in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Founded in 2016, our mission is to uplift youth, textile graduates, and general students through industry-aligned skill development courses. We bring experts, real-world projects, and practical tools under one roof to ensure you learn what the job market demands.

  • Industry-Relevant Courses — Focused on practical skills for today's job market.
  • Expert Instructors — Learn from professionals with real-world experience
  • Hands-On Learning — Every course is designed with live projects and practical exercises.
  • Career Support — Guidance on freelancing, entrepreneurship, and job placement in Cods Clothing Co. and other reputed companies.
